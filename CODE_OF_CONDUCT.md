# Code of Conduct

Bastion uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and tradeoffs.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
